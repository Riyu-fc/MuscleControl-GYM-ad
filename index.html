ABOUT US

Welcome to MuscleControl Gym, where fitness meets community! We are a small gym dedicated to empowering individuals to achieve their health and fitness goals.

MuscleControl Gym was founded by Reylyn Infante and Joey Pasco, a couple who share a passion for weightlifting and fitness. What started as a shared hobby turned into a vision for a business that could inspire and help others reach their fitness goals. Reylyn and Joey both had successful careers but often felt that their true passion lay in fitness and the empowering feeling that comes from strength training. They spent countless hours at the gym, not just working out but constantly learning about effective techniques, muscle-building strategies, and the benefits of a balanced and healthy lifestyle.

In November of last year, Reylyn and Joey decided to turn their shared passion into something more meaningful. They had a dream of creating a place where anyone, regardless of fitness level, could feel comfortable and begin their fitness journey. MuscleControl Gym was envisioned as more than just a workout facility. They wanted it to be a place that would bring together people from all walks of life, fostering a community that valued hard work, consistency, and the joy that comes from self-improvement.

They began their journey with a humble space but poured their heart and soul into building it. Reylyn and Joey personally designed every corner, selected every piece of equipment, and ensured that MuscleControl Gym was a place where people could train without judgment and find encouragement from others around them. Today, MuscleControl Gym has already become a special part of the community. Reylyn and Joey continue to work hard to make it a place that reflects their journey, where everyone can feel empowered to pursue their fitness dreams.

Join us at MuscleControl Gym, and discover a place where you can control your strength, sculpt your physique, and unleash your full potential. Together, let's build a healthier, happier you!
